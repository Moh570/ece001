The 5 largest international companies topped the artificial intelligence "inventions" in 2019

The American software giant Microsoft ranked first in the world among the most companies in the number of patents related to artificial intelligence as of January 2019,
according to the report of the German market research company (IPlytics)

According to the report, Microsoft obtained the lead after it owned 18,365 patents, while the American company "IBM" came second with 15,046 patents.

In third place, the Korean company Samsung Electronics came with 11,243 patents in the field of artificial intelligence.

And the American company Qualcomm ranked fourth with 10178 patents, then Google fifth with 9,536 patents.
